Our Facilities

India Energy Week

India Energy Week is the pre-eminent event for experts in the traditional and non-traditional energy industry from around the world.

FIPI Annual Convention

The convention provides a platform to the students to interact, compete and share knowledge with each other and also an opportunity to listen to the industry experts, academicians etc.

ADIPEC

Abu Dhabi International Petroleum Exhibition and Conference(ADIPEC) provides eminent platforms for the oil and gas industry for business and information exchange, attracting global energy ministers, global CEOs and leading decision makers.

Tanzania Energy Congress

Tanzania Energy Congress brings together energy ministers from around the world, policymakers, oil & gas companies, service providers, industry experts and leaders.

FIPI Journal

FIPI issues monthly magazines to showcase recent developments, ups and downs in the Oil and Gas Industry.

BP Energy Outlook

BP's Energy Outlook 2022 is focused on three main scenarios to explore the energy transition.
